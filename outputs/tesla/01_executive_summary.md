# Tesla - Executive Summary

# Tesla, Inc. Company Overview

## Basic Information
- **Company Name:** Tesla, Inc.
- **Legal Name:** Tesla, Inc. (formerly Tesla Motors)
- **Stock Ticker:** TSLA
- **Founded:** 2003
- **Headquarters:** Austin, Texas (previously Palo Alto, California)
- **Industry/Sector:** Automotive, Clean Energy, Technology
- **Website:** tesla.com

## Company Description
Tesla is a pioneering electric vehicle manufacturer and clean energy company that designs, develops, manufactures, and sells high-performance electric cars, solar panels, solar roof tiles, energy storage systems, and electric vehicle charging infrastructure. The company is known for its innovative approach to sustainable transportation and energy solutions.

## Key Facts
- **Founders:** Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning
- **Current CEO:** Elon Musk (joined in 2004, became CEO in 2008)
- **Public/Private Status:** Publicly Traded (NASDAQ)
- **Geographic Presence:** Global (North America, Europe, Asia)
