## Executive Summary

TIM Brasil is Brazil's third-largest mobile telecommunications operator with 62.1 million subscribers and approximately 24.8% market share. The company has distinguished itself through operational excellence, achieving the highest EBITDA margin (48%) among Brazilian operators and the lowest churn rates in the industry (0.7% postpaid). With €6 billion in planned investments for 2025-2027 focused on 5G expansion and AI integration, TIM is positioned as Brazil's efficiency leader and fastest-growing operator in terms of ARPU. The company was ranked the #1 most sustainable company in Brazil in 2024 by B3's Corporate Sustainability Index.

### Key Investment Highlights
- **Operational Excellence:** 48% EBITDA margin, best in class
- **Lowest Churn:** 0.7% postpaid churn rate, industry-leading
- **5G Leadership:** 64% population coverage, most extensive in Brazil
- **ARPU Growth:** R$32.3 ARPU, highest and fastest-growing
- **ESG Leader:** #1 in Brazil's ISE Sustainability Index (17 consecutive years)
- **Strategic Plan:** €6B investment 2025-2027, targeting 3% revenue CAGR
