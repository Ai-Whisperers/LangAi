## Executive Summary

Claro Brasil is the second-largest telecommunications operator in Brazil, commanding approximately 34% of the mobile market with 85 million mobile subscribers. As a subsidiary of América Móvil (controlled by Carlos Slim), Claro benefits from substantial financial backing, with a planned investment of US$7.7 billion (R$40 billion) from 2024-2029 focused on 5G and fiber expansion. The company reported R$50 billion in net revenue for 2024 with 13.2% EBITDA growth in Q3 2024, demonstrating strong operational performance despite intense competition from market leader Vivo and aggressive third-place player TIM.

### Key Investment Highlights
- **Market Position:** #2 mobile operator with 34% market share
- **Revenue Growth:** 6-9.4% YoY revenue growth in mobile services
- **Infrastructure:** 11.1 million homes passed with fiber, 317 cities with 5G
- **Pay-TV Leadership:** 41.6% market share in pay-TV segment
- **Parent Support:** US$7.7B committed investment through 2029
- **B2B Growth:** Embratel/Claro Empresas growing 28-45% in enterprise services
