## Executive Summary

Claro Paraguay is the third-largest mobile operator in Paraguay with approximately 26% market share and an estimated 1.8-2.0 million subscribers. As a subsidiary of América Móvil, Claro benefits from regional expertise and parent company resources but faces strong competition from market leader Tigo (Millicom) and second-place Personal (Telecom Argentina). The company has not yet launched commercial 5G services, with spectrum auctions expected in 2025. Claro Paraguay operates in a challenging competitive environment where Tigo dominates with ~50% market share, making differentiation and strategic positioning critical for growth.
