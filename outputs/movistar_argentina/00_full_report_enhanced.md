## Executive Summary

Movistar Argentina is being acquired by Telecom Argentina (Personal) for US$1.245 billion, marking the exit of Telefónica from the Argentine market. With approximately 28% mobile market share and 16-21 million customers across mobile and fixed services, Movistar represents a significant consolidation opportunity. The deal, signed February 24, 2025, is under regulatory review by CNDC and ENACOM. Post-acquisition, the combined entity will control approximately 61% of mobile subscribers and up to 80% of residential broadband in some districts. This report focuses on Movistar's pre-acquisition profile and transaction implications.
