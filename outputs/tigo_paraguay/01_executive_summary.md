## Company Description
Tigo Paraguay is a telecommunications company providing mobile and fixed-line services, internet, and TV. The company has been in the Paraguayan market for 32 years, indicating a strong brand presence and customer loyalty. Being part of the larger international group Millicom might provide access to resources and expertise that enhance its competitive position.
