## Executive Summary

Personal Argentina, the mobile brand of Telecom Argentina S.A., is one of Argentina's leading telecommunications operators with 21.6 million mobile subscribers and approximately 33% market share. In a transformative move, Telecom Argentina acquired Telefónica Argentina (Movistar) in February 2025 for US$1.245 billion, pending regulatory approval, which would consolidate the Argentine market to two major players. Despite hyperinflationary challenges, Personal leads in network quality with the fastest 5G speeds (432 Mbps) and highest availability (97.5%) among Argentine operators. The company reported net income of P$1,033 billion in FY24, with continued investment in 5G and fiber infrastructure.

### Key Investment Highlights
- **Market Consolidation:** Movistar acquisition pending (US$1.245B) - transformative
- **Network Leadership:** Fastest 5G (432 Mbps), highest availability (97.5%)
- **Subscriber Growth:** 21.6M mobile subscribers (+3% YoY)
- **Convergence Strategy:** Mobile, broadband, cable TV integration
- **5G Investment:** 260+ sites deployed, 100 MHz of 3.5 GHz spectrum
- **Dividend Yield:** ~2.5% with special distributions planned
