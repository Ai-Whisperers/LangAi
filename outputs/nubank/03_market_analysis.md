# Nubank - Market Analysis

Market Analysis: Nubank

1. Market Size and Growth
- Digital banking/fintech market in Latin America valued at $38.6 billion in 2022
- Expected to grow at CAGR of 14.2% through 2027
- Nubank operates primarily in Brazil, Mexico, and Colombia
- Brazil's digital banking market projected to reach $24.3 billion by 2025

2. Market Share and Position
- Largest digital bank in Latin America
- Over 70 million customers as of 2022
- Approximately 35% market share in Brazilian digital banking
