## 1. Executive Summary

Nike, Inc. remains a dominant global athletic apparel and footwear company with a strong market position, despite experiencing slight market share declines and revenue challenges in recent years. The company continues to leverage its brand strength, innovative product design, and global marketing strategies to maintain leadership in the consumer discretionary sector. While facing competitive pressures and economic headwinds, Nike's diversified brand portfolio and established global presence position it for potential future growth and adaptation.

## 2. Company Overview

### History and Mission
Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation founded in 1964, originally known as Blue Ribbon Sports. The company was renamed Nike in 1971, derived from the Greek goddess of victory. Nike's mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world, with the famous philosophy that "if you have a body, you are an athlete."

### Leadership
