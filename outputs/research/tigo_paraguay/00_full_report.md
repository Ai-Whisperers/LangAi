# Tigo Paraguay

## Company Overview

**Industry:** Telecommunications
**Founded:** 1992
**Headquarters:** Asuncion, Paraguay

Tigo Paraguay is a telecommunications company offering unlimited mobile services, including data, WiFi, and calls, catering to both individuals and businesses.

- **Revenue:** $1.37 billion (2025)
- **Employees:** Not available

- Market leader in Paraguay with 54.67% market share
