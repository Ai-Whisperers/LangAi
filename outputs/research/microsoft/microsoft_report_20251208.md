## 2. Company Overview

Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in Albuquerque, New Mexico. The company's mission is to empower every person and organization to achieve more through innovative technology solutions. Currently led by CEO Satya Nadella, Microsoft has transformed from a primarily software-focused company to a comprehensive technology and cloud computing leader.
