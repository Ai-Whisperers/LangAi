## 2. Company Overview

Grupo Bimbo is a multinational bakery product manufacturing company headquartered in Mexico. With a presence in 35 countries across four continents, it operates 227 bakeries and markets more than 100 brands, including Thomas', Sara Lee, and Entenmann's. The company's expansive product range and global footprint underscore its position as a leader in the baking industry.
