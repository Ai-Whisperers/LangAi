## 1. Executive Summary

ExxonMobil is a leading global integrated oil and gas company, producing approximately 3% of the world's oil and 2% of global energy. With a market capitalization of $509.73 billion as of May 2024 and annual operating revenue of $349.6 billion, the company remains a dominant force in the energy sector. The organization continues to leverage its extensive chemical and energy expertise to drive innovation and sustainability across multiple industries.
