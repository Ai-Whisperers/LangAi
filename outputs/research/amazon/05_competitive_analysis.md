## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- Dominant market position in e-commerce with extensive logistics network
- Leading cloud computing platform (AWS) with strong growth and profitability

- Strong financial performance with $59.2 billion net income in 2024

- High operating expenses in AWS segment impacting margin growth

### Opportunities

- International market expansion, particularly in emerging economies
- Growth in cloud computing adoption by enterprises
- Expansion of Prime membership benefits and services

### Threats
- Intensifying competition in cloud computing from Microsoft, Google, and others
- Regulatory scrutiny in multiple jurisdictions
- Economic downturns affecting consumer discretionary spending
- Rising operational costs and supply chain challenges
