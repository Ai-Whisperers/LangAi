- **Hybrid Technology Leadership**: Toyota maintains a dominant position in hybrid vehicle technology

## SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- **Market-leading hybrid technology** with 21.1% global growth in hybrid sales
- **Strong financial performance** with $256.18B market cap and healthy profit margins
- **Diversified electrification portfolio** allowing flexibility in market approach
- **Global manufacturing presence** with record sales across multiple regions

### Weaknesses
- **Limited BEV market presence** with electric vehicles accounting for less than 0.1% of total sales
- **Revenue decline** from $307.60B in 2023 to $305.26B in 2024
- **Slower adoption of full electrification** compared to some competitors

### Opportunities
- **Growing hybrid market** with 36% sales growth in Q2 2025 and 22% market share
- **Electrification transition** with increasing consumer acceptance of electrified vehicles
- **Technology leadership position** in hybrid systems applicable to future technologies

### Threats
- **Rapid BEV market growth** potentially outpacing Toyota's current electric vehicle strategy
- **Regulatory pressure** for faster electrification in key markets
- **Competitive pressure** from both traditional automakers and new EV manufacturers
