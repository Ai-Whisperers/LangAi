## 1. Executive Summary
AT&T, a telecommunications giant with roots in the Bell Telephone Company, has transformed from a monopolistic telephone service to a diversified communications and media conglomerate. The company has maintained its position as a leading telecommunications provider, with annual revenues around $122.3 billion in 2024, while strategically expanding into media, satellite TV, and wireless services. Despite facing intense market competition, AT&T continues to leverage its extensive network infrastructure and broad service portfolio.
