## 1. Executive Summary
The Coca-Cola Company remains a dominant global beverage leader with over 200 brands across 200+ countries. In 2024, the company achieved annual revenue of $47.061 billion, representing a 2.86% year-over-year increase. With a market share of nearly 48% in the U.S. non-alcoholic beverage market, Coca-Cola continues to demonstrate resilience and strategic adaptation in a competitive global marketplace.

## 2. Company Overview
### History
- Founded in Atlanta, Georgia, by pharmacist Asa Griggs Candler in 1892
- Incorporated after securing complete ownership of the original Coca-Cola formula
- First bottling agreement signed in 1899
- Expanded from a single soft drink to a global beverage portfolio

### Mission
"Refresh the world and make a difference" by providing diverse beverage options and creating positive global impact through sustainable business practices.

### Leadership
- Global system employs approximately 700,000 people
- Operates through multiple geographic segments: Eurasia and Africa, Europe, Latin America, North America, Asia Pacific, Bottling Investments, and Global Ventures
