## 9. SWOT Analysis

### Strengths
- **Market Leadership**: 43% global CSD market share and 48% U.S. market share
- **Brand Portfolio**: Over 200 brands across multiple beverage categories
- **Pricing Power**: Demonstrated ability to achieve 11% price/mix growth
- **Financial Performance**: Consistent revenue growth and strong market capitalization
- **Global Presence**: Established worldwide distribution and brand recognition

### Weaknesses
- **Volume Growth**: Modest 2% volume growth suggests potential market saturation challenges
- **Category Dependence**: Heavy reliance on carbonated soft drinks in a health-conscious market

### Opportunities
- **Total Beverage Strategy**: Expansion into healthier beverage categories (waters, teas, functional drinks)
- **Emerging Markets**: Continued growth potential in developing markets
- **Innovation**: Product diversification beyond traditional sodas

### Threats
- **Health Trends**: Continued consumer shift away from sugary beverages
- **Competitive Pressure**: Intense competition from PepsiCo and emerging beverage brands
- **Regulatory Environment**: Potential sugar taxes and health-related regulations
