## Company Overview

Founded in 1957 and headquartered in Chile, Grupo Quiñenco is a conglomerate with a broad portfolio that includes investments in leading companies across various industries. It operates both domestically and internationally, showcasing a strategic focus on banking, financial services, manufacturing, and more. The Luksic Group, one of South America's largest family offices, controls Quiñenco, emphasizing its significant footprint in the region's economic landscape.

The company also completed the sale of 5% of Nexans for US$310 million, further bolstering its financial position. With a net income of Ch$842 billion in 2023, the company has demonstrated a return to normalcy after exceptional years, supported by a revenue of A$13.07 Billion in 2024, albeit a slight decrease from A$13.15 Billion in 2023.
