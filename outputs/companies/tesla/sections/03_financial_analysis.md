## Financial Analysis

### Revenue Performance (2024)

**Total Revenue:** $97.69 billion (+$917 million or ~1% growth from 2023)

**Segment Breakdown:**
- **Automotive:** $77 billion (-6% from $82.4 billion in 2023)
- **Energy Generation and Storage:** $10 billion (+67% from $6 billion in 2023)
- **Services and Other:** ~$10.69 billion (implied)

### Profitability Metrics (2024)

**Gross Profit Margins:**
- Overall: 17.9% (down from 18.2% in 2023)
- Automotive (including regulatory credits): 18.4% (down from 23.3% in 2023)
- Energy: 26.2% (up from 23.3% in 2023)
- Services: 5.8% (down from 6.2% in 2023)

**Operating Profit Margin:** 7.2% (down from 9.2% in 2023)

**Net Income:** 
- 2024: $7 billion (7.3% net margin)
- 2023: $15 billion (15.5% net margin) - included one-time $5.7 billion non-cash tax benefit
- Adjusted comparison: ~53% decline in net income

**EBITDA:**
- 2024: $13.027 billion (-3.9% from 2023)
- 2023: $13.558 billion (-21.3% from 2022)
- Q3 2025: $14.708 billion (annualized estimate)
- 5-year average (2020-2024): $11.505 billion
- 5-year low: $4.273 billion (December 2020)

### Financial Health Indicators

**Positive Signals:**
- Energy segment showing strong growth (67% revenue increase)
- Energy business contribution to gross profit increased from 6% to 15%
- Maintained positive cash flow and profitability despite challenges
- Strong delivery growth in Q2 2025 (29% year-over-year)

**Concerns:**
- Declining automotive revenues and margins due to aggressive pricing/incentives
- Compressed operating margins indicating operational pressure
- Significant net income decline (even adjusting for 2023 tax benefit)
- Declining EBITDA trend over past two years
- Services segment showing low margins (5.8%)
