## Market Position

**Market Ranking:**
Claro Paraguay operates as one of three major mobile operators in Paraguay, though specific market share data is not provided in the available information.

**Competitive Position:**
- One of three primary mobile network operators in Paraguay
- Part of the largest telecommunications group in Latin America (América Móvil)
- Benefits from regional scale and Claro brand recognition across 18 countries

**Geographic Presence:**
- Nationwide coverage across Paraguay
- "Extensive coverage" referenced for 4G LTE network
- Strategic frequency allocation supporting both urban and rural coverage (700 MHz Band 28 particularly effective for rural areas)

**Industry Positioning:**
- Established incumbent operator (16+ years in market)
- Full-service mobile operator with multi-generation technology
- Positioned for next-generation 5G services with 3.5 GHz spectrum allocation
