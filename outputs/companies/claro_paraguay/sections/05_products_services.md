## Products & Services

**Core Service Offerings:**

1. **Mobile Voice Services**
   - 2G GSM network (1900 MHz PCS band)
   - 3G UMTS network (1900 MHz PCS band)
   - Prepaid and postpaid plans

2. **Mobile Data Services**
   - 3G UMTS data services
   - 4G LTE high-speed data
   - Mobile internet packages
   - Social media access bundles

3. **Device Sales**
   - Latest smartphone offerings
   - Device financing options (implied through postpaid services)

4. **Value-Added Services**
   - International roaming within Claro network across Latin America
   - Mobile financial services (Billetera/wallet services referenced)
   - Digital payment solutions

**Technology Infrastructure:**

- **GSM:** 1900 MHz (PCS band)
- **UMTS/3G:** Band 2 (1900 MHz PCS)
- **LTE/4G:** 
  - Band 4 (1700/2100 MHz AWS 1)
  - Band 28 (700 MHz APT)
- **5G:** 3.5 GHz band allocated (not yet commercially launched)

**Key Differentiators:**
- Part of pan-Latin American Claro network enabling seamless regional roaming
- Dual-band LTE strategy providing both capacity (Band 4) and coverage (Band 28)
- Integration with América Móvil's regional infrastructure and services
- Comprehensive service portfolio from basic connectivity to digital financial services
