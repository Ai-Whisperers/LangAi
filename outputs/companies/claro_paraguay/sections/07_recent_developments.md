## Recent Developments

**Network Infrastructure (2024):**
- **August 2024:** Addition of LTE Band 4 (1700/2100 MHz AWS 1) and Band 28 (700 MHz APT) to network infrastructure, indicating ongoing network densification and coverage expansion
- Continued 4G LTE optimization and expansion

**5G Preparation:**
- **As of December 2024:** No commercial 5G launch yet completed
- **National Telecommunications Plan (PNT) 2021-2025:** Framework established for 5G deployment
- **Target:** 50% population coverage by 2025
- **Spectrum:** 3.5 GHz band assigned for mobile 5G services
- Claro Paraguay positioned for future 5G deployment within national framework

**Market Competition:**
- **2024:** Nubicom announced agreement with Remsa for potential market entry, introducing uncertainty about fourth operator entry
- Continued three-player competitive dynamics with Tigo and Personal

**Operational Developments:**
- Implementation of Ericsson ServiceNow support reporting platform for business operations
- Focus on digital transformation initiatives
- Expansion of financial services (Billetera/mobile wallet) operations
- Revenue and tax management system enhancements

**Strategic Initiatives:**
- Network modernization and capacity expansion
- Preparation for 5G commercial launch
- Digital services expansion beyond traditional connectivity
- Integration with América Móvil's regional strategy and sustainability initiatives
