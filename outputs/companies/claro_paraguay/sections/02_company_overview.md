## Company Overview

**Legal Information:**
- **Legal Name:** AMX Paraguay S.A.
- **Operating Brand:** Claro Paraguay
- **Founded:** 2008 (3G network launched November 2007, suggesting pre-operational setup)
- **Headquarters:** Paraguay
- **Parent Company:** América Móvil, S.A.B. de C.V.

**Business Description:**
Claro Paraguay is a full-service mobile telecommunications operator providing voice, data, and value-added services to the Paraguayan market. The company operates as part of the Claro brand family, which spans 18 countries across Latin America and provides seamless roaming services within the América Móvil network.
