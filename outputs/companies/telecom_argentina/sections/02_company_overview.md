## Company Overview

**Legal Information:**
- **Legal Name:** Telecom Argentina S.A.
- **Founded:** 1990
- **Headquarters:** Argentina
- **Company Type:** Public company
- **Stock Ticker:** TEO (also listed as TECO2)
- **Industry:** Telecommunications Service Providers

**Business Description:**
Telecom Argentina is a comprehensive telecommunications provider offering mobile telephony, fixed-line services, broadband internet, and pay television services across Argentina. The company operates the "Personal" mobile brand and provides services to both individual consumers and business clients through prepaid and postpaid models.
