## Products & Services

**Main Offerings:**

*Mobile Services:*
- 2G, 3G, and 4G LTE networks
- Limited 5G deployment in Argentina
- Prepaid and postpaid plans
- Mobile devices and data plans
- Branded as "Personal" for mobile telecommunications

*Fixed-Line Services:*
- Traditional voice telephony
- Business communication solutions

*Broadband Internet:*
- Fiber-to-the-Home (FTTH) technology
- HFC (Hybrid Fiber-Coaxial) cable infrastructure
- High-speed internet services

*Television Services:*
- Pay television offerings
- Delivered through cable infrastructure

**Key Differentiators:**
- Integrated service portfolio (quad-play capability)
- Advanced fiber infrastructure deployment
- Established brand recognition through "Personal" mobile brand
- Expanded national coverage following TMA acquisition
- Technology diversity across multiple network generations

**Technology Stack:**
- Multi-generation mobile network (2G through limited 5G)
- Fiber optic infrastructure (FTTH)
- HFC cable network
- Integrated telecommunications platform supporting voice, data, and video services
