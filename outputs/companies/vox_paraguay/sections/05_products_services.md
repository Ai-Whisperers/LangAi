## Products & Services

**Core Offerings:**

1. **Voice Services**
   - Mobile voice calls (2G/3G)
   - National and international calling

2. **Messaging Services**
   - SMS (Short Message Service)
   - Basic text messaging capabilities

3. **Data Services**
   - Mobile data on 2G/3G networks
   - Data-focused positioning despite limited network capabilities

4. **Service Model**
   - No-contract required (prepaid focus)
   - Flexible, commitment-free plans
   - Targeted at price-sensitive segments

**Technology Stack:**
- **GSM Technology:** Pioneer in Paraguay (1999)
- **2G Network:** Voice and basic data
- **3G Network:** Enhanced data capabilities
- **Notable Absence:** 4G/LTE and 5G infrastructure

**Service Differentiators:**
- **Historical:** First GSM provider in Paraguay (1999)
- **Current:** No-contract flexibility
- **Pricing:** Likely positioned as budget/value option
- **State Backing:** Government ownership may provide service continuity assurance

**Limitations:**
- Outdated network technology compared to competitors
- Limited data speeds and capacity
- No apparent premium or innovative service offerings
- Minimal digital transformation or value-added services
