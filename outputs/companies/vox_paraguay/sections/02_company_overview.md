## Company Overview

**Legal Details:**
- **Legal Name:** VOX Comunicaciones S.A.
- **Former Name:** Hola Paraguay S.A. (1999-2010)
- **Founded:** May 12, 1999 (commercial operations launch)
- **Headquarters:** Asunción, Paraguay
- **Website:** www.vox.com.py

**Business Description:**
VOX operates as an MVNO in Paraguay's telecommunications sector, providing mobile voice, SMS, and data services primarily on 2G and 3G networks. The company distinguishes itself through flexible, no-contract service offerings targeting price-sensitive consumers. As a state-owned enterprise under COPACO, VOX represents Paraguay's government presence in the mobile telecommunications market.

**Ownership Structure:**
- **Parent Company:** COPACO (Compañía Paraguaya de Comunicaciones) - State-owned
- **Acquisition Date:** August 2010
- **Acquisition Price:** Approximately USD $3 million
