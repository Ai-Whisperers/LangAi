## Executive Summary

América Móvil, S.A.B. de C.V. is one of Latin America's largest telecommunications conglomerates, founded in 2000 and headquartered in Mexico. The company operates as a comprehensive telecommunications provider across 26 countries in the Americas and Europe, offering mobile voice and data services (2G through 5G), fixed-line telephony, broadband internet, pay TV, and enterprise solutions. With operations spanning multiple continents and serving millions of subscribers, América Móvil has established itself as a dominant force in the Latin American telecommunications market.

The company demonstrates strong financial performance with consistent revenue growth and healthy profitability metrics. As of Q4 2024, América Móvil maintains a robust financial position with a net debt to EBITDA ratio of 1.6x and EBITDA margins approaching 40%. The company operates major brands including Telmex, Telcel, and Claro across its markets, serving approximately 50 million subscribers in Mexico alone. With a credit rating of 'A-' from Fitch (with positive outlook) and recent successful capital market activities including a €650 million notes issuance, América Móvil continues to strengthen its market position while expanding its service offerings and technological capabilities.
