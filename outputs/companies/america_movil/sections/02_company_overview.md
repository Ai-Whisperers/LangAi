## Company Overview

**Legal Information:**
- **Legal Name:** América Móvil, S.A.B. de C.V.
- **Founded:** 2000
- **Headquarters:** Mexico
- **Industry:** Telecommunications Services
- **Parent Company:** Independent (publicly traded)

**Business Description:**
América Móvil is a comprehensive telecommunications corporation providing integrated services across mobile and fixed-line platforms. The company operates as one of the largest mobile network operators in the Americas, with significant presence in Europe through its Austrian operations. The company's business model encompasses both consumer and enterprise segments, offering end-to-end telecommunications solutions including voice, data, internet, television, and digital content services.
