## Products & Services

**Main Offerings:**

1. **DSL Internet Services**
   - Traditional broadband connectivity
   - Legacy technology facing obsolescence

2. **Limited Fiber Optic Services**
   - Next-generation connectivity (limited deployment)
   - Suggests infrastructure investment constraints

3. **International Gateway Services**
   - Critical infrastructure for international connectivity
   - Potential strategic asset and revenue source

4. **International Voice Termination**
   - Wholesale telecommunications services
   - Declining revenue stream due to VoIP and OTT services

5. **Transit Services**
   - Carrier-grade telecommunications routing
   - B2B wholesale services

**Key Differentiators:**
- Incumbent infrastructure ownership
- International gateway control
- Established wholesale relationships
- Historical market presence (75+ years)

**Technology Stack:**
- Predominantly legacy copper infrastructure (DSL)
- Limited fiber deployment
- International gateway infrastructure
