## Products & Services

**Core Service Offerings:**

**Mobile Services:**
- Voice and data services (2G, 3G, 4G LTE)
- 5G services (limited markets, early deployment phase)
- Prepaid and postpaid plans
- Mobile financial services and mobile money solutions

**Fixed Services:**
- Broadband internet (HFC - Hybrid Fiber-Coaxial, FTTH - Fiber to the Home)
- Cable TV and pay-TV services
- Entertainment products and content

**Digital Services:**
- Mobile financial services platform
- Digital payment solutions
- Entertainment and content streaming

**Key Differentiators:**

1. **Triple "A" Strategy:**
   - **Affordable:** Pricing tailored to emerging market consumers
   - **Accessible:** Wide network coverage in underserved areas
   - **Available:** Consistent service availability and reliability

2. **Converged Service Model:** Integrated mobile, fixed broadband, and entertainment services create customer stickiness

3. **Mobile Financial Services:** Addressing banking gaps in emerging markets where traditional financial infrastructure is limited

4. **Market-Specific Approach:** Deep understanding of local market dynamics and customer needs

**Technology Stack:**
- Multi-generation mobile network infrastructure (2G through 5G)
- Hybrid fiber-coaxial and fiber-to-the-home broadband technologies
- Focus on innovation and cost control in network deployment
- Heavy investment model supporting continuous infrastructure expansion
