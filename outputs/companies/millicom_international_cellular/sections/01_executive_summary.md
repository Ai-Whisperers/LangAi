## Executive Summary

Millicom International Cellular S.A., operating under the **Tigo** brand, is a leading telecommunications and digital services provider focused exclusively on Latin American emerging markets. Founded in 1990 and headquartered in Luxembourg, the company has established itself as a dominant player in the region, serving over 46 million customers across nine markets as of December 2024. The company holds either the first or second position among operators in eight of its nine Latin American markets, with particularly strong leadership in Guatemala.

The company demonstrated robust financial performance in 2024, achieving record equity free cash flow of $777 million (exceeding its target) and reducing leverage to 2.42x. Full-year revenue reached $5.80 billion with 2.5% year-over-year growth, while Q4 2024 EBITDA hit $618 million, up 11% year-over-year. Millicom's business model centers on its "Triple A" strategy—making services Affordable, Accessible, and Available—positioning it well for continued growth in underserved emerging markets. The company targets approximately $750 million in equity free cash flow for 2025 and plans to distribute $500 million to shareholders while retaining funds for deleveraging and strategic initiatives.
