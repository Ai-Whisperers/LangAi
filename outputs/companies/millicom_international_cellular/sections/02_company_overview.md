## Company Overview

**Legal Details:**
- **Legal Name:** Millicom International Cellular S.A.
- **Founded:** 1990
- **Headquarters:** Luxembourg
- **Brand Name:** Tigo
- **Parent Company:** N/A (Independent entity)
- **Industry:** Telecommunications

**Business Description:**
Millicom operates as a converged telecommunications and digital services provider specializing in emerging markets across Latin America. The company's business model emphasizes providing essential connectivity and digital services with a strong focus on customer acquisition and retention in underserved markets representing a total population under mobile license of close to 300 million.

**Key Leadership:**
- **Chief Executive Officer:** Marcelo Benitez (appointed June 1, 2024)
- **Former Chief Financial Officer:** Sheldon Bruha (stepped down April 15, 2024)
- **Board Members:** Include Justine Dimovic (proposed new member, 2024) and other experienced telecommunications executives with expertise in listed companies and European market development

**Historical Context:**
The company underwent significant transformation, with remaining businesses including satellite TV operations and broadband licenses contributed to American Satellite Network Inc. In 2008, Millicom acquired Amnet Telecommunications Holding Limited, the leading provider of broadband and Cable TV services in Central America, significantly expanding its regional footprint.
