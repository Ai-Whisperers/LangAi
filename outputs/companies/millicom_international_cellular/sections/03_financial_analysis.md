- **Q4 2024 Revenue:** $1.43 billion
- **Full-Year Equity Free Cash Flow:** $777 million (record performance, excluding tower sales; $728 million excluding tower sales in alternative reporting)
- **Q4 2024 Equity Free Cash Flow:** $236 million
- **Q4 2024 EBITDA:** $618 million (11% year-over-year increase)
- **Q4 2024 Operating Profit:** $373 million
- **Q4 2024 Cash CapEx:** $162 million
- **Leverage Ratio:** 2.42x (improved from previous periods)

**Growth Trends:**
- Revenue growth of 2.5% demonstrates steady expansion despite challenging emerging market conditions
- EBITDA growth of 11% year-over-year in Q4 indicates improving operational efficiency
- Equity free cash flow exceeded targets, showing strong cash generation capabilities
- The company experienced an expected dip in 2023 revenue and profit, but underlying business fundamentals improved heading into 2024

**Financial Health Indicators:**
- **Positive:** Record equity free cash flow generation, reduced leverage, stable CapEx, and strong EBITDA margins
- **Concerns:** Potential negative impacts on 2025 equity free cash flow due to FX risks in Bolivia, Colombia, and Paraguay
- **Capital Allocation:** $500 million planned for shareholder distribution from $750 million equity free cash flow, with remainder allocated to deleveraging and strategic projects

**2025 Guidance:**
- **Equity Free Cash Flow Target:** Approximately $750 million (3% year-over-year increase)
- **Expected Trends:** Rising EBITDA with stable CapEx contributing to equity free cash flow growth
