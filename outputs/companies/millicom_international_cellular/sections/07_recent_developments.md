## Recent Developments

**Strategic Acquisitions:**
- **Telefónica Ecuador Acquisition (2025):** $380 million deal strengthening South American footprint and expanding customer base

**Leadership Changes:**
- **New CEO:** Marcelo Benitez appointed as Chief Executive Officer effective June 1, 2024, bringing distinguished career experience with Millicom
- **CFO Transition:** Sheldon Bruha stepped down as Chief Financial Officer on April 15, 2024

**Financial Milestones:**
- **Record Cash Flow:** Achieved record equity free cash flow of $777 million in 2024, exceeding targets
- **Deleveraging Success:** Reduced leverage ratio to 2.42x, improving financial flexibility
- **Shareholder Returns:** Announced $500 million distribution to shareholders from 2024 cash flow

**Operational Improvements:**
- Strong Q4 2024 performance with 11% EBITDA growth year-over-year
- Underlying business improvements despite 2023 revenue dip
- Stable CapEx management while maintaining network investment

**Future Outlook:**

**2025 Targets:**
- Equity free cash flow target of approximately $750 million (3% growth)
- Continued EBITDA expansion with stable CapEx
- Integration of Telefónica Ecuador acquisition
- Balance between shareholder returns and strategic reinvestment

**Strategic Priorities:**
- Further deleveraging to strengthen balance sheet
- Selective strategic projects and acquisitions
- Expansion of digital and mobile financial services
- 5G rollout in key markets
- Continued focus on customer acquisition and retention in emerging markets

**Risk Factors:**
- Foreign exchange volatility in Bolivia, Colombia, and Paraguay
- Economic and political instability in Latin American markets
- Regulatory changes and compliance requirements
- Competitive pressure from regional and international players
