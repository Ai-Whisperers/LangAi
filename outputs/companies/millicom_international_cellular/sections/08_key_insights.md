## Key Insights

1. **Strong Financial Performance with Record Cash Generation:** Millicom achieved record equity free cash flow of $777 million in 2024, exceeding targets and demonstrating robust cash generation capabilities. The company reduced leverage to 2.42x while maintaining growth investments, positioning it well for both shareholder returns ($500 million distribution) and strategic initiatives.

2. **Dominant Market Position in Latin American Emerging Markets:** With 46+ million customers and first or second position in 8 of 9 markets, Millicom has established a defensible competitive moat in Latin America. The company's focus on underserved emerging markets with a population of nearly 300 million under mobile license provides significant long-term growth runway.

3. **Successful Converged Services Strategy:** The integration of mobile, fixed broadband, cable TV, and mobile financial services creates a comprehensive ecosystem that increases customer lifetime value and reduces churn. This converged approach differentiates Millicom from single-service competitors and addresses multiple needs of emerging market consumers.

4. **Strategic Expansion Through Telefónica Ecuador Acquisition:** The $380 million acquisition of Telefónica Ecuador strengthens Millicom's South American footprint and demonstrates management's commitment to strategic growth through selective M&A. This expansion aligns with the company's core competency in emerging market telecommunications.

5. **Triple "A" Strategy Drives Sustainable Growth:** Millicom's focus on making services Affordable, Accessible, and Available specifically targets the unique needs of emerging market consumers. This strategy, combined with mobile financial services addressing banking gaps, positions the company to capture growth as Latin American economies develop and digital adoption accelerates.
