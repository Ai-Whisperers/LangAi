## Recent Developments

**Regulatory & Spectrum (2024):**
- **February 28, 2024:** CONATEL granted renewal of 700 MHz spectrum band, ensuring continued 4G operations and 5G readiness
- **Ongoing:** No 3G network shutdown announced as of December 2024, maintaining service continuity for legacy devices

**Infrastructure Investments:**
- **2021 Commitment:** $47 million joint investment with Claro for 4G license renewal and 5G preparation infrastructure
- **Tower Monetization:** Sale of wireless towers to ATIS Group, part of Millicom's asset-light strategy while maintaining network operations

**Parent Company Developments (Millicom):**
- **2024 Financial Performance:** Record equity free cash flow of $728 million
- **Leadership Change:** Marcelo Benítez assumed CEO role in 2024, replacing Mauricio Ramos
- **Regional Expansion:** Acquired Telefónica's operations in Ecuador and Uruguay (2025), strengthening Latin American footprint
- **Strategic Focus:** Emphasis on ESG integration, leverage reduction, and free cash flow generation
- **African Exit:** Completed divestiture of African operations by 2022, concentrating resources on Latin America

**Service Enhancements:**
- **Fiber Optic Expansion:** Continued rollout of Tigo Internet Hogar fiber services
- **Tigo Money Growth:** Platform serving 2.2 million active clients with expanding financial services
- **Customer Engagement:** Ongoing "El Dia E" campaigns for direct customer interaction and feedback

**5G Outlook:**
- **Status:** Commercial 5G services not yet launched as of 2024
- **Preparation:** Infrastructure investments and spectrum holdings position company for near-term deployment
- **Timeline:** No specific launch date announced, but regulatory approvals and network investments indicate readiness

**Strategic Initiatives:**
- **Digital Inclusion:** Positioning as "engine of digital inclusion and transformer of realities"
- **Customer-Centric Approach:** Continued focus on customer service excellence (Master Chef-style engagement programs)
- **Network Modernization:** Ongoing transition from legacy technologies to 4G/5G infrastructure
- **Ecosystem Development:** Integration of telecommunications, entertainment, and financial services
