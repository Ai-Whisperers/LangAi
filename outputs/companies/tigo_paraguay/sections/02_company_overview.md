## Company Overview

**Legal Structure:**
- **Legal Name:** Telefonica Celular del Paraguay S.A. (Telecel)
- **Operating Brand:** Tigo Paraguay
- **Founded:** 1991
- **Headquarters:** Paraguay
- **Parent Company:** Millicom International Cellular S.A. (Luxembourg-based, NASDAQ: TIGO)

**Business Description:**
Tigo Paraguay is a full-service telecommunications provider offering mobile voice and data services, fixed broadband internet, cable television, and mobile financial services. The company operates as Paraguay's leading telco service provider and is part of Millicom's Latin American operations, which serve approximately 46 million mobile customers across 11 countries.
