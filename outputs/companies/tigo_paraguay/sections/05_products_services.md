## Products & Services

**Mobile Services:**
- **2G GSM:** Legacy network still operational
- **3G UMTS:** Launched July 2007 (Huawei infrastructure), no shutdown announced as of December 2024
- **4G LTE:** Launched April 2016 on AWS spectrum, expanded with 700 MHz band (renewed February 2024)
- **5G Preparation:** Infrastructure investments committed, commercial launch pending
- **Service Types:** Prepaid (70%) and Postpaid (30%) plans

**Fixed Services:**
- **Tigo Internet Hogar:** Fiber optic internet for residential customers
- **Fixed Broadband:** High-speed internet connectivity
- **Fixed Telephony:** Traditional and IP telephony services
- **Private Links & MPLS:** Enterprise connectivity solutions

**Television Services:**
- **Tigo TV:** Cable television with premium channel offerings
- **IPTV:** Internet protocol television services
- **DTH/Satellite:** Direct-to-home satellite television

**Digital Financial Services:**
- **Tigo Money:** Mobile financial services platform with 2.2 million active users
- **Money Transfers:** Domestic and international remittances
- **Bill Payments:** Utility and service payment processing
- **Microcredits:** Quick access to small loans
- **Access Points:** Phone-based and 4,000+ physical outlet network

**Enterprise & Cloud Services:**
- Cloud services (SaaS, PaaS, IaaS)
- Data centers and managed services
- Business Process Outsourcing (BPO)
- Systems engineering and consultancy
- Call center services
- Corporate connectivity solutions

**Technology Infrastructure:**
- **Network Equipment:** Huawei (3G infrastructure provider)
- **Spectrum Holdings:** AWS band, 700 MHz band
- **Coverage Technologies:** Wi-Fi, WiMax, Small cells, LTE-M
- **Service Delivery:** OTT platforms, mobile apps, converged services
