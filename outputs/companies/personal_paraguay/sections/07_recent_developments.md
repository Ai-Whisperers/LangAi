## Recent Developments

**Technology Evolution:**

- **5G Preparation (2024):** Paraguay's CONATEL updated the National Frequency Allocation Plan (PNAF) to accommodate 5G services, reallocating spectrum between 3300-3700 MHz. Spectrum auctions planned for 2024 with ambitious goal of 50% population coverage by 2025.

- **3G Network Status:** While no specific shutdown date announced, regional trends suggest phase-out planning is underway as the company focuses resources on 4G and future 5G networks.

**Service Expansion:**

- **DTH Launch:** Introduction of Direct-to-Home satellite television services with both prepaid and postpaid packages, expanding entertainment offerings and reaching customers beyond fiber optic coverage areas.

**Financial Performance:**
