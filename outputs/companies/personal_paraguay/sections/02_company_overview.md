## Company Overview

**Legal Structure:**
- **Legal Name:** Núcleo S.A.
- **Operating Brand:** Personal Paraguay
- **Founded:** June 24, 1998
- **Headquarters:** Asunción, Paraguay
- **Parent Company:** Telecom Argentina S.A.
- **Industry:** Telecommunications

**Business Description:**
Personal Paraguay operates as a comprehensive telecommunications and digital services provider in the Paraguayan market. The company has expanded beyond its original mobile telephony focus to become a multi-service operator offering:
- Mobile telecommunications (2G GSM, 3G UMTS, 4G LTE)
- Fixed broadband services via fiber optic networks
- IPTV and satellite television (DTH) services
- Mobile financial services through Personal Envíos SA
- Corporate telecommunications solutions
