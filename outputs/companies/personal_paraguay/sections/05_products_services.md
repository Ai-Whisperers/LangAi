## Products & Services

**Mobile Telecommunications:**

- **2G GSM:** Legacy network for basic voice and SMS services
- **3G UMTS:** Launched in 2008
  - Initial speeds up to 3.6 Mbps
  - Operating on UMTS B2 (1900 MHz PCS) and B5 (850 MHz) frequency bands
  - Phase-out potentially planned for near future following regional trends
- **4G LTE:** Commercial launch February 8, 2013
  - Early market entry providing competitive advantage
  - Current primary data network
- **5G:** Not yet launched as of December 2024
  - Awaiting spectrum auction
  - Planned spectrum: 3300-3700 MHz range

**Fixed Broadband Services:**

- **Fiber Optic Internet:** High-speed residential and business connectivity
- Positioning as alternative to mobile-only connectivity
- Supports convergent service bundles

**Television Services:**

- **IPTV:** Internet-based television services
- **DTH (Direct-to-Home) Satellite Television:**
  - Launched with both prepaid and postpaid packages
  - Monthly subscription options
  - Expands service reach to areas without fiber infrastructure
